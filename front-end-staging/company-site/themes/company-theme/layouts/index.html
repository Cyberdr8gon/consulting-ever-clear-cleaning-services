Solar Panel Cleaning

Solar panels get much less effective when they get dirty. What looks like even just a small coating of dust makes solar panels less effective, decreasing the amount of energy they can produce for you. What is worse, is that the companies that sell you these only test completely clean solar panels when projecting energy production of the panels.

$5 per panel
